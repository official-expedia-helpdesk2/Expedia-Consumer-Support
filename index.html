The AI assistant helps connect you to the right live person faster based on your issue.

12. Get Support from Hotel/Airline via Expedia

15. Contact Expedia for Travel Insurance Claims

Use the customer service number to be routed to a partner insurance provider if you purchased coverage through Expedia.
